03
What we heard

The opportunity is bigger than one channel.

Nikko Blu has existing digital activity, internal capacity, and past paid ads spend. The system needs clarity: what is producing ROI, what campaigns should be adjusted, whether tracking is trustworthy, and whether the website is ready to convert the attention being generated.

Marketing needs prioritization

Kim’s team needs a clear filter for what actually moves revenue, what supports trust, and what can be ignored.

Tracking needs cleanup

GA4, Shopify analytics, Google Ads, Search Console, and Google Business Profile should be reconciled before paid spend is treated as scalable.

Website matters

Traffic eventually hits the site. The store path, local info, mobile experience, and trust signals need to be part of the audit.
